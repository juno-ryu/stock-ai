## 1. Introduction

The service is a mock stock investment web application for novice investors, including MZ generation and employed people. It helps users safely build investment experience and improve investment knowledge by providing real-time stock quotes, investment analysis reports, and real-time news feeds.

## 2. Goals

*   Provide a safe and easy-to-use mock investment platform for novice investors.
*   Provide customized investment information and analysis for MZ generation investors.
*   Provide a quick and convenient stock trading environment for employed people.

## 3. Target Audience

*   Novice investors who are new to stock investment
*   MZ generation investors interested in trendy investment
*   Employed people who want to invest as a side job

## 4. Features

*   Real-time stock quotes: Provide real-time stock price information.
*   Mock trading: Provide a mock trading environment for users to practice investing.
*   Investment analysis report: Provide AI-based investment analysis and customized reports.
*   Real-time news feed: Provide real-time stock market news.
*   Interest stock registration and notification: Allow users to register interest stocks and receive notifications.

## 5. User Journey

1.  A novice investor signs up for the service and learns how to invest through the tutorial.
2.  An MZ generation investor receives customized investment information and analysis and discovers new investment ideas.
3.  An employed person checks stock quotes quickly during lunch time and makes mock trades.

## 6. UI/UX

*   Simple and intuitive interface for novice investors
*   Trendy design to appeal to MZ generation
*   Quick and easy navigation for employed people

## 7. Future Considerations

*   Add social trading feature to allow users to follow other investors' portfolios.
*   Add automatic trading feature to allow users to set up automatic trading rules.
*   Expand investment products to include stocks, funds, and other assets.
